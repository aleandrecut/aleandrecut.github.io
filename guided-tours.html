Guided Tours

Ready to discover Rodnei Mountains?

Meet Ana, our guides coordinator! She is one of the most cheerful and adventurous people you will ever meet and she will make sure that you will have the most wonderful hiking experiences.

Ana is also a Scouts leader and has a special way with children. She studied Tourism and got her certification as a mountains guide last year.

Please note that prices for guided tours start at 90 lei and vary depending on the length and difficulty of the trail. You can get more information and book a tour at the phone number in our contact section or by sending us an email.
