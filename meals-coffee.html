The Hiker`s Den supports local producers

There is no fix menu at the den and that`s because we value the hard work of our local producers and try to adapt our list of dishes periodically depending on what we can buy from them. You can request a list of the available dishes and prices when you arrive at the den or you can call us for more information.

Free tea and coffee

Our guest are our friends and we don`t charge friends for coffee when they come over, do we?

When we started building the den, we wanted more than just a business, we wanted a home, both for our guests and for ourselves. So as long as you stay at The Hiker`s Den, feel free to serve yourself a cup of coffee as if you were at your own place.
