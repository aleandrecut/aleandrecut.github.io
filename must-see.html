Must See

The Edelweiss

This rare flower is considered to be a symbol of alpinism, but be careful: it is also a protect species so don`t try to collect it. Finally, here`s a tip: they love growing near the the rocks, so now you know where to look for them.

The Daffodil Glade from Saca Mountain

Another protected species, the wild daffodil, covers an entire field. We highly recommend going to see it towards the end of May to have more chances of seeing the flowers in bloom.

A sunrise from the ridge

Wake up early in the morning, take your backpack and be ready for some magic. Don`t worry about the fog, it will make it look even more like a fairytale.

The Glory and the Brocken Spectre

If you are lucky enough, you may even spot the Glory or the Brocken Spectre, some very rare optical phenomenons that only happen under very strict circumstances. So if you are up in the mountains and see a big shadow surrounded by a halo standing just in front of you don`t get scared, it`s only Physics! 

The Chamois

You thought the edelweiss and the daffodils were hard to find? Try spotting this one! With it`s ability to climb rocks, this protected species of goat-antelope are the winners of hide-and-seek. Many have tried to photograph them, but only few succeeded. Could you be one of them?
