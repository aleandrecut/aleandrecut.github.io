Our Story

Who are we?

Dana
The Coffee Addict

Dana can`t start her day without a good cup of coffee! But that`s good for us and for you too. She makes sure that we never run out of this magic liquor and she`s ready to share it with everybody. She`s also a great cooker and a certified photographer.

Marius
The Enthusiast

Had a bad day? Don`t worry, Marius is here to make you forget about it and spread some happiness. He is the one that will help you install your tent, lit the fire and sing a lullaby all along. Caution: he`s smile is contagious!

Anastasia

There is nobody who knows the trails better than she does. She is our number one guide and most trusted nanny during the trips. She says she was born hiking

Miruna
The Mom

Life gets a bit reckless when you live in the mountains, especially when you have your head in the clouds! Miruna makes sure to bring us with our feet on the ground and keep everything clean and organized. Rules are rules even at the den, but don`t worry, she`s a cool mom.

Why are we here?

We are all hiking enthusiasts and we want to help others discover the magic of this activity. In 2018 we decided that we had to do more for our cause so we partnered up with the administration of Rodnei Mountains National Park in order to create The Hiker`s Den, a place in the heart of the mountains where wanderers and hikers could feel at home.

It took us a lot of hard work to get here and we still encounter challenges everyday - living in the mountains is not easy - but now that the den has been open for almost 2 years already we cannot imagine ourselves anywhere else but here.

Our guests` kindness, their smiles and their curiosity to discover all the hidden wanders of Rodnei Mountains add a little spark to everything that we do and encourage us to keep going.

Ready to meet us and start your own adventure? Take a look at what what you can see and do here.
